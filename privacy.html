Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 10, 2026

Twin Unified Studios LLC respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form, we may collect your name, email address, selected service category, message, and any other information you choose to provide.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, discuss potential projects, provide commission updates, manage client communications, and maintain business records.

FormSubmit

This website uses FormSubmit to process contact form submissions and deliver them to our business email. Information you submit through the form is transmitted through FormSubmit before it reaches us.

Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who help operate our website or communications, when required by law, or when necessary to protect our rights, users, business, or services.

Cookies and Analytics

This maintenance page does not currently use cookies, advertising pixels, or analytics tools. If we add those tools later, we will update this Privacy Policy as needed.

Data Retention

We keep contact form submissions for as long as reasonably needed to respond to inquiries, manage client relationships, maintain records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No website, email system, or online transmission is completely secure.

Adults-Only Website and Minors' Privacy

This website is intended only for adults who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you are under 18, do not submit the contact form or provide personal information through this website. If we learn that we have collected personal information from someone under 18, we will take reasonable steps to delete it. If you believe a minor has submitted information through this website, please contact us.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information you have submitted, subject to legal and business recordkeeping requirements.

State and International Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights under applicable law. We will respond to privacy requests as required by applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
