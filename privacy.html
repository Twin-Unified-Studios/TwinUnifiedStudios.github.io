Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Twin Unified Studios collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website.

  • 1. What we collect We may collect your name, email address, and other voluntarily submitted information through our contact form, or other forms that require voluntary information gathering.
  • 2. How We Use It We use your information strictly to respond to inquiries and improve our services. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • 4. What we don't do We do not:

    - Sell or Share any of your data with third parties.

    - Use the data for tracking, advertising, or profiling.

  • 5. How it's stored The information you provide is sent directly to our email inbox via the contact form. We do not store this data in a website database. Any local storage (used only for display settings like dark/light mode) does not contain personal data and can be cleared by the user. If other forms are used, they are also locally stored and is used solely for gathering information, as well as helping our business evolve.
  • 6. Cookies We may use functional cookies to improve your browsing experience (e.g., remembering dark or light mode). These cookies do not track personal data. For more information, see our Cookies Notice for more.
  • 7. Your rights You can request to view, update, or delete your information by contacting us directly.

We are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your information and will revise this policy as our practices evolve.
