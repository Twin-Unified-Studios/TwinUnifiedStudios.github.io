Privacy Policy

Twin Unified Studios LLC – InvoBaseOS

Effective Date: April 14th, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Twin Unified Studios LLC ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in connection with the InvoBaseOS website and application. InvoBaseOS is designed with a privacy-first, offline-first approach. We intentionally minimize data collection and design the product around local control, security, and reduced exposure of business-sensitive information.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The InvoBaseOS website and related landing pages
  • The InvoBaseOS application and related product pages
  • Any future optional connected features, only if and when such features are introduced

3. Information We Collect

Website

  • Theme preference only, for light and dark mode display
  • Policy banner acknowledgement preference, if you choose to dismiss the notice
  • Information you voluntarily submit through the contact form, such as your name, email address, selected service, and project details

We do not intentionally collect through normal website use:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Behavioral tracking data
  • Unnecessary cookies
  • Personal data for profiling or ad targeting

InvoBaseOS Application

  • Application data is intended to remain stored locally on your device
  • Application data is intended to remain encrypted
  • During normal offline use, invoice records, user records, and action logs are not transmitted to us

4. Cookies and Local Preferences

The website uses strictly necessary local preference storage only to remember your visual theme setting, such as light mode or dark mode, and whether you have dismissed the policy acknowledgement banner. These settings are stored in your browser and are used only to improve your viewing experience and avoid repeatedly showing the same notice. They are not used for advertising, profiling, tracking, or analytics.

5. How We Use Information

Because InvoBaseOS is designed to minimize collection, our use of information is limited. Theme preference and policy acknowledgement data are used solely to preserve your selected visual theme across visits and remember whether you dismissed the policy notice. Contact form information is used to review and respond to your inquiry, discuss requested services, and maintain reasonable business records related to your request. We do not use website preference information to identify you personally, monitor your behavior, or sell insights about your activity.

6. Data Ownership and Local Control

You retain ownership and control of your locally stored InvoBaseOS data. We do not claim ownership of your invoice records, local logs, account data, or business information created within the application. Under normal offline use, we do not receive or host that data.

7. Data Security

Security and privacy are a core design priority for InvoBaseOS. Depending on the current version and feature set, security-oriented measures may include:

  • Encryption of stored business and application data
  • Tamper detection or tamper-aware safeguards
  • Role-based access controls such as Primary Admin, Admin, and Standard access levels
  • Re-authentication for certain sensitive or destructive actions
  • Action logging and audit-oriented event tracking
  • Multi-step verification or warning flows for destructive actions

8. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not intentionally share personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties through the normal use of the website or offline application. However, certain website features may rely on third-party service providers that process information as needed to provide those features.

Contact Form

If you submit the contact form, your submission may be processed by FormSubmit or a similar form delivery provider so that your message can be delivered to us by email. The information you choose to submit may include your name, email address, selected service, and project details. Please avoid submitting sensitive personal, financial, health, or confidential business information through the website contact form unless it is necessary for your inquiry.

Embedded Maps

The main website may include an embedded Google Maps frame to display a general location. When you load a page containing that map, Google may receive technical information from your browser, such as your IP address, device/browser information, and interaction data, according to Google's own policies. Twin Unified Studios LLC does not use the embedded map for advertising profiling or behavioral tracking.

9. Future Optional Features

Future product updates may include optional connected services, such as cloud-based sync, backup, transfer, or account-linked features. If those features are introduced, they may involve additional data handling. Any such changes will be disclosed through an updated Privacy Policy, and any optional feature will be identified as such where reasonably applicable.

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users in the European Economic Area may have rights that can include:

Because InvoBaseOS is designed for local-first use and because we generally do not receive or process your local application data during normal offline use, many of these rights are supported by the fact that your data remains under your own control on your own device.
